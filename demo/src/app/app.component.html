Options

Option Type Description
descriptionstringExpands toast from pill to blob.
actionGoeyToastActionAction button with optional success label.
timing.displayDurationnumberExpanded display duration in milliseconds.
fillColorstringCustom blob background color.
borderColorstringCustom border color.
borderWidthnumberBorder width in px.
typeColorsPartial<Record<GoeyToastType, string>>Override tone colors per toast type.
radius.pillnumberCollapsed pill corner radius in px.
radius.bodynumberExpanded body corner radius in px.
radius.actionnumber | stringAction button border radius.
springbooleanEnable spring-style morphing and squish.
bouncenumberSpring intensity (0.05 to 0.8).
